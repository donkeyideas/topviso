Lumen Finance / Strategy

The Top Viso playbook.

Reference guide. Six-step implementation, do's and don'ts, how every metric in Top Viso connects to business outcomes.

§ 01

Six-step implementation

01

Audit keywords

Start in Keywords tab. Identify everything you rank for and everything a competitor ranks for that you don't. Cross-platform gaps are the quickest wins.

02

Map intents

Intent Map groups your keywords into clusters. Identify weak and missing clusters. Strong clusters deserve CPPs; weak ones need Optimizer edits.

03

Optimize metadata

Edit in Optimizer. Watch ASO score per field. Use AI variants for stubborn descriptions. Always leave 15%+ keyword field free for iteration.

04

Test creative

Creative Lab. Never ship a new visual without A/B testing. 2-week tests with 3 variants. Let winners auto-rollout.

05

Route reviews

Reviews+ pipes themes into Linear. Respond to all 3-star-or-below reviews within 48 hours. It measurably raises your average.

06

Measure, adjust

Attribution tells you what's actually working. Kill leaking paid channels. Double down on high-incrementality organic paths.

Do

  • ✓Leave 15%+ of the iOS keyword field free for weekly iteration.
  • ✓Respond to every review within 48 hours — measurably lifts average rating.
  • ✓A/B test creative before every version release.
  • ✓Seed authoritative sources (Reddit, Wirecutter) to shape LLM recommendations.
  • ✓Localize the 5 largest markets before spreading thin across 20.

Don't

  • ×Stuff keywords into your subtitle — readability affects CVR more than density.
  • ×Chase every competitor's keyword — some aren't your positioning.
  • ×Treat "organic installs" as free. Measure incrementality.
  • ×Ship a major version update without preparing new screenshots.
  • ×Respond to reviews with copy-paste. AI drafts are a start, not the shipped text.
